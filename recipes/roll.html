Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is generally two slices of bread with filling in between.

In this case the filling is cheese, lettuce and tomato.

Ingredients

  • Bread (sliced)
  • Cheese (sliced)
  • Tomato (sliced)
  • Lettuce (sliced)

Steps

  1. Place slices of roll alongside each other.
  2. Add ingredients.
  3. Put one slice of roll onto the other.
  4. Enjoy!
